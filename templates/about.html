About Me

Hi there! I'm Nikita , a passionate web developer and fashion enthusiast based in India.

I created Baggykooks to merge my love for code and creativity with the world of fashion. As someone who adores styling bags and staying on top of trends, I felt inspired to build a platform where bag lovers and fashion-forward individuals can explore, shop, and express their personal style — all in one place.

My Motive Behind Baggykooks

The idea for Baggykooks came from noticing a gap — there were so many beautiful bags out there, but no single stylish theme dedicated entirely to them.

So, I designed Baggykooks as a modern, fully block-based WordPress theme that’s:

✅ Sleek and responsive
✅ Customizable without coding
✅ Tailored for bloggers, brands, and everyday style lovers

My goal? To help creators showcase bags with beauty and purpose — turning every look into a statement.
